Welcome to ACL2020!

The conference comprises of mainly two types of material: pre-recorded and live. You can watch the pre-recorded material anytime before or during the conference, whereas the live events have a fixed timing. Following are the main elements of the conference:

Schedule
You can find the times for all the live sessions using the Schedule, where you can choose your timezone and find live sessions that work for you.
Plenary Sessions
Plenary Sessions include Keynote Talks (pre-recorded talk and live Q&A), live award ceremonies (Lifetime Achievement, Distinguished Service, Test of the Time and Best Paper) and ACL Business Meeting (pre-recorded talks and live discussions).
Main Conference Papers
Each paper will have a pre-recorded talk (long papers 12 minutes and short papers 7 minutes), two live Q&A video sessions (on Zoom) at different times, and a linked RocketChat channel (where you can chat anytime with the author).
System Demonstrations
Each demo will have a pre-recorded 12-min talk, two live Q&A video sessions (on Zoom) at different times, and a linked RocketChat channel.
Student Research Workshop
Each paper will have a pre-recorded 12-min talk, two live Q&A video sessions (on Zoom) at different times, and a linked RocketChat channel.
Tutorials
There are 8 tutorials (all included under conference registration). Some tutorials are fully pre-recorded whereas some have pre-recorded talks and live sessions. Visit the tutorials page for more information.
Workshops
There are 19 workshops (all included under conference registration). Some workshops are fully pre-recorded whereas some have pre-recorded talks and live sessions. Visit the workshops page for more information.
Sponsors
Meet our sponsors by visiting the sponsor page.
Chat
You can interact with other conference attendees using various RocketChat channels. When you access RocketChat for the first time, click on 'Login via ACL2020 account' and your ACL2020 credentials will be automatically used to log you in to RocketChat.
Socials
You can also meet new people and other participants using various social events organised throughout the week.
Help
If you have questions about planning your time or navigating, look under Help for answers to frequently asked questions and technical support.
Social Media
Share papers, workshops, and demos by using the hashtag #ACL2020
